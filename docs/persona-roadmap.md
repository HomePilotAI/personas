# Persona Roadmap

This project includes ten personas designed to appeal to a broad audience and encourage viral adoption.  They are listed below in order of priority:

1. **Creator Muse** – a creative assistant that helps brainstorm and inspire new ideas.
2. **Style Muse** – a fashion and aesthetics consultant offering style advice and curation.
3. **Secretary Pro** – an efficient secretary who manages schedules, reminders and communications.
4. **Researcher** – a scholarly assistant capable of searching publications, reading papers and summarising findings.
5. **Personal Trainer** – a fitness expert providing personalised workouts and wellness advice.
6. **Room Stylist** – an interior designer suggesting room layouts, décor and colour palettes.
7. **Storyteller** – a narrative companion who creates engaging stories based on prompts.
8. **Exam Coach** – an educational coach generating practice questions and study plans.
9. **Mindfulness Coach** – a mindfulness guide offering meditation and stress‑management techniques.
10. **General Doctor** – a general practitioner persona providing non‑diagnostic health advice.
